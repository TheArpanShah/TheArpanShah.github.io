Why Nest?

Have you ever wondered how much energy you are wasting from your thermostat alone? Did you know that a thermostat could account for up to half of your home's energy consumption? How Does Nest Work?

The Nest Thermostat has a Nest Sense Technology which observes your day-to-day routine and maintains your HVAC schedule automatically. The Nest Thermostat comes with three temperature sensors to get a more precise measurement, a humidity sensor to gauge mositure, motion and light sensors that detect at a 150 degree angle, and WIFI that gathers your local area's weather information. With data from these sources, Nest sets a schedule with auto-activate times each day along with an auto-away feature that engages when you are not home. In additon, Nest can be manually modified from the Thermostat, online, or from an app on your phone. Just turn the temp up and down for your comfort and the Nest will start to learn your habits automatically. The Nest Leaf appears when you choose a temperature that saves energy. The Nest Thermostat works in place of most modern thermostats.
